About

TARA Mobile Technologies is a mobile application development company. We develop Mobile Applications for the Android, iOS and Windows Mobile Platforms. We also specialize in cross device mobile web development.

TARA employees have a unique blend of industry and academic experience. This gives us the ability to deliver value to our clients by blending leading edge technologies and futuristic research.

Scientific Advisors

Raj Reddy

Dabbala Rajagopal "Raj" Reddy is an Indian-American computer scientist and a winner of the prestigious Turing Award. He is one of the early pioneers of Artificial Intelligence and has served on the faculty of Stanford and Carnegie Mellon for over 40 years. He was the founding Director of the Robotics Institute at CMU. He was one of the founders of the American Association for Artificial Intelligence and was its President from 1987 to 1989. Reddy was a co-chair of the President's Information Technology Advisory Committee (PITAC) from 1999 to 2001. He serves on the International Board of Governors of Peres Center for Peace in Israel.

Jaime Carbonell

Jaime Guillermo Carbonell is a computer scientist who has made seminal contributions to the development of natural language processing tools and technologies. His extensive research in machine translation has resulted in the development of several state-of-the-art language translation and artificial intelligence systems. He earned his B.S. degrees in Physics and in Mathematics from MIT and did his Ph.D. under Dr. Roger Schank at Yale University. He joined Carnegie Mellon University as an Assistant Professor of Computer Science and has lived in Pittsburgh since then.

Directors

Matt Brocchini

Matt Brocchini is a Silicon Valley veteran with 20 years experience driving innovation and growth in start-ups and large enterprises. He was a founder or early exec in six tech startups, with one IPO and one acquisition. Before joining Market Brew, Matt was General Manager of Nimblefish Technologies, the world's leading Marketing Engine platform. Nimblefish was acquired by RR Donnelley, the $10B integrated communications company. Earlier Matt founded Helium.com, an innovation consulting firm that designed and built ground-breaking products for clients ranging from Adobe to the San Francisco 49ers, and was Director of Product Management at Oracle Corporation. Matt is a founding board member and current Board Chair of the Institute for Applied Tinkering, a non-profit whose mission is fostering innovation in children.

Praveen Garimella

Praveen Garimella plays a key role in technological innovation and in the design of Social, Mobile, Analytics and Cloud based software systems at Tara. He is a doctoral student in Computer Science at International Institute of Information Technology Hyderabad. He created computer and smart phone based activity-monitoring technology to understand how people learn in online learning environments. He has over 8 years of experience in the design and delivery of learning by doing environments for both face-to-face and online settings. In 2006 he built a prototype called PCtvt in collaboration with Intel for people with no formal education. He studied Software Engineering in Carnegie Mellon Silicon Valley Campus in 2004 and e-Business Technologies in Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh in 2005.

Careers

Full-Time Employment

Looking for a challenging environment where there are no limits to exploring your creativity? Look no more. TARA hires fresh college graduates and trains them in our proprietary mobile technology development process.

Upon successful completion of training you get to choose which project you would like to work on from the portfolio of projects available based on fit. We encourage our employees to think (and develop) out of the box in a very challenging and stimulating environment. Given our broad spectrum of services from contract mobile/web development projects to skunkworks kind for projects for companies big and small, you will be sure to find projects you will be passionate about.

If you are interested in doing an internship at TARA, please email your resume to careers@taramt.com

Internship

TARA provides internships for students pursuing Masters degrees in Information Technology and related disciplines. We also provide limited internships for exceptional engineering students. The internships can be full time and or part time.

Internships typically last from 3 to 6 months. Full time interns are always paid a stipend. During the internship, our interns work on a variety of projects ranging from research, model development to system design, development and implementation. The projects are structured to impart new skills to the interns.

If you are interested in doing an internship at TARA, please email your resume to careers@taramt.com
